Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Deutsch · English

Kontiva is a private, on-device household budgeting app. Your financial data never leaves your iPhone.

The short version

Kontiva does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. There are no accounts, no servers, no cloud, no analytics, and no third-party trackers. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What Kontiva stores, and where

Everything you enter — income, costs, bills, savings goals, debts, your household name and profile picture, and your preferences — is stored only on your device, inside an encrypted vault. The vault is sealed with AES-256-GCM, using a key derived from your personal code (PBKDF2).

Kontiva has no backend and does not connect to the internet to function. We, the developers, never see your data and have no technical way to access it.

The only thing stored outside the encrypted vault is your chosen interface language and colour theme (saved in the app's local preferences so they apply on the lock screen). These contain no personal or financial information.

Your code, and recovery

Your data is protected by a numeric code, and optionally Face ID / Touch ID. There is no recovery. If you forget your code, your data cannot be accessed — not by you, and not by us. This is intentional and is the price of true privacy.

Backups

If you create a backup, it is an encrypted file that you control, protected by a separate backup passphrase you choose, saved to a location you pick. We never receive it.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

None. Kontiva contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. It does not use the device's Advertising Identifier and does not track you across apps or websites.

During the TestFlight beta

While Kontiva is distributed through Apple's TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs and basic usage information in order to deliver the beta, as described in Apple's own privacy policy. This is handled by Apple, not by Kontiva — the app itself still collects nothing.

Children

Kontiva is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
