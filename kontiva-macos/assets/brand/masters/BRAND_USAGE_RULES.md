# Kontiva Brand Usage Rules

## Core Rule

The logo concept is approved. Keep it intact.

Claude Code, designers, or future contributors may generate platform-specific icon formats from the approved source files, but must not redesign the logo.

## Approved Accent

```text
#E11D2E
```

Do not replace this with another red unless explicitly approved.

## Logo Rules

- Keep proportions intact.
- Do not stretch.
- Do not crop.
- Do not blur.
- Do not recolor.
- Do not add mascots.
- Do not add decorative effects that change the brand.
- Do not recreate the wordmark with a random font.

## App Packaging

Allowed generated outputs:

- `.ico` for Windows.
- `.icns` for macOS.
- Tauri PNG icon sizes.
- Installer/banner usage if visually faithful.

Generate these from the approved source assets only.

## Website/App Usage

Recommended placements:

- app icon,
- desktop shortcut,
- app sidebar/header,
- onboarding/welcome screen,
- settings/about screen,
- website header,
- download page,
- README/documentation.
