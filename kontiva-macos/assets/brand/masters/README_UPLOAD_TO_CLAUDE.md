## Important

The SVG files remain in the full brand ZIP as production/vector masters. Claude may not accept SVG as project files, so these PNG/PDF/MD files are the Claude-friendly alternatives.

## Brand Direction

Kontiva uses a professional geometric `K` monogram with ledger/accounting balance lines and a restrained Swiss red accent.

The approved visual direction should feel:
- professional,
- simple,
- elegant,
- eye-catching,
- calm,
- premium,
- private,
- Swiss-compatible,
- trustworthy enough for salary statements, bills, and tax documents.

Avoid:
- piggy banks,
- coins,
- dollar signs,
- mascots,
- fake bank crests,
- shield/security-software look,
- excessive gradients,
- loud green,
- childish budget-app language.
