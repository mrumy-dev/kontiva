# Kontiva Brand Assets

Kontiva is a serious Swiss personal finance desktop app for budgeting, bills, salary, private documents, and tax organization.

## Chosen Direction

The selected logo direction combines:
- a geometric `K` monogram,
- ledger/accounting balance lines,
- a restrained Swiss red accent,
- a dark premium app tile,
- a serious modern wordmark.

The mark should feel calm, private, structured, and trustworthy. It should not feel playful, childish, or like a generic budget app.

## Files

- `kontiva-logo-concept-sheet.png`: approved concept sheet from the generated logo exploration.
- `kontiva-icon.svg`: primary dark app icon master.
- `kontiva-icon-light.svg`: light/monochrome-style app icon.
- `kontiva-mark.svg`: standalone K ledger mark.
- `kontiva-wordmark.svg`: website/header wordmark.
- `kontiva-wordmark-dark.svg`: dark mode wordmark.

PNG exports may be generated from the SVG masters for app stores, desktop shortcuts, favicons, installer icons, and website usage.

## Palette

- Charcoal: `#121A22`
- Off-white: `#F6F7F8`
- Warm light: `#F8F7F4`
- Swiss red accent: `#E11D2E`
- Soft border: `#DCE1E5`

## Usage Rules

- Keep the red accent small and intentional.
- Do not add mascots, coins, piggy banks, dollar signs, or generic finance symbols.
- Do not turn the logo into a shield or bank crest.
- Keep spacing generous and typography calm.
- Use the icon alone for desktop shortcuts and app icons.
- Use the wordmark for website headers, installer screens, and documents.
